# Vibe Examples and Translation Tables

Load this file only when you need examples, translation tables, or vocabulary for `$vibe-matcher`.

## Translation Examples

### Example: "Edgy but still professional"

- Colors: dark charcoal + a single hot accent + clean white
- Typography: heavy headings, restrained body
- Layout: asymmetric grid with a few intentional breaks
- Motion: fast and sharp (150-250ms), no slow floaty transitions
- Risk: can hurt trust if overdone; keep a calm content structure

### Example: "Premium wellness"

- Colors: warm off-white + muted natural green + terracotta accent
- Typography: elegant serif for headings + readable sans for body
- Layout: generous whitespace, centered rhythm, soft curves
- Motion: slow and calm (400-600ms), gentle fades

## Domain-Aware Tables

### "Edgy" (by domain)

| Domain | Colors | Typography | Layout | Interactions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Professional portfolio | dark + single bold accent | heavy sans headings | asymmetric but readable | snappy, crisp |
| SaaS | high contrast | angular/geometric | broken-grid moments | glitch/contrast micro-moments |
| E-commerce | black/red or metallic | tight tracking, all-caps sparingly | diagonal rhythm | aggressive hover states |

### "Trustworthy" (by domain)

| Domain | Colors | Typography | Layout | Interactions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fintech | navy + conservative neutrals | clean sans + careful numerals | strict grid | smooth, predictable |
| Healthcare | soft blue/white + green accents | readable, calm | clear hierarchy | gentle, reassuring |
| Legal | deep blues + burgundy | traditional serif | formal structure | minimal, dignified |

### "Premium" (by domain)

| Domain | Colors | Typography | Layout | Interactions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Luxury goods | black/white + gold accent | elegant serif + thin sans | whitespace-heavy | slow, deliberate |
| Tech | deep blacks + metallic | modern sans | sleek, minimal | polished, smooth |
| Creative services | mono + bold accent | serif + modern sans | sophisticated asymmetry | subtle, refined |

## Vocabulary (use to disambiguate)

- Energy: calm / balanced / intense
- Formality: casual / professional / formal
- Innovation: conservative / modern / cutting-edge

## Common Anti-Patterns

- Generic mapping (\"premium\" always equals black/white/gold).
- Ignoring audience: playful for kids is not playful for enterprise.
- Style over function: vibe that hurts readability or navigation.
